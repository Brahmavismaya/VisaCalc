About Us

About VisaCalc

Free immigration calculators for UK, Canada, Australia & the US — designed to empower applicants globally.

Who We Are

VisaCalc is an independent platform built by developers who believe that understanding your immigration options shouldn't require paying a consultant thousands of dollars just to get a preliminary estimate. Immigration rules are published openly by governments — we make them accessible.

What We Offer

Five free, regularly updated calculators:

  • UK Spouse Visa Cost Calculator — exact fee breakdown including IHS surcharge
  • Canada CRS Score Calculator — Express Entry score with draw cutoff comparison
  • Australia PR Points Calculator — skilled migration points test
  • US Green Card Wait Time Estimator — by country and preference category
  • H-1B Visa Timeline Estimator — processing times by service center

Our Approach

Every calculator is built directly from official government sources — Gov.uk, IRCC Canada, Australian Home Affairs, and USCIS. We update our tools whenever rules or fees change and display a "Last verified" date on every tool so you always know how current the information is.

All calculations happen entirely in your browser. We never collect, store, or transmit your personal data. There is no account, no signup, and no cost.

How to Use These Tools

VisaCalc is built to help you explore your options, understand where you stand, and walk into any professional consultation already informed. Our calculators reflect official government rules — they're a powerful starting point. For advice tailored to your personal circumstances, a licensed immigration solicitor (UK), Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC), Registered Migration Agent (RMA), or US immigration attorney can guide you further.

Immigration rules change frequently. Always verify critical details with the relevant official government source before making application decisions.
