Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum points score for Australian skilled migration?

The minimum points required to be eligible to lodge an Expression of Interest (EOI) via SkillSelect is 65 points. However, most subclass 189 invitations are sent to applicants scoring 80–95+ points.

Which visas use the Australia points test?

The points test applies to subclass 189 (Skilled Independent), subclass 190 (Skilled Nominated), and subclass 491 (Skilled Work Regional). Subclass 491 often invites lower scores (65–75) due to regional requirements.

How often does Australia send SkillSelect invitations?

SkillSelect invitation rounds are typically held monthly, though frequency can vary. Subclass 189 invitations are most competitive; state-nominated streams (190) vary by state and occupation.

How can I improve my Australia PR points score?

Key improvements: achieve Superior English (IELTS 8.0+ all bands) for +10 pts, gain Australian work experience, complete Australian study, or explore state/regional nominations which add 5–15 bonus points.

What was the lowest invited score for Australia subclass 189 recently?

In recent SkillSelect rounds (late 2025), the lowest invited score for subclass 189 (all occupations) was 85–90 points. Some occupation-specific rounds have lower cutoffs.
